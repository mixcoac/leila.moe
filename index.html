about me

I'm a 16-year-old trans content creator from Mexico City.

I was born at the age of 0 in a hospital that still exists, I enjoy listening to avant-garde music and reheating Little Caesars Pizza.

I'm a writer, developer, engineer, archivist, and economist of sorts; hence why I prefer to address myself as a "content creator". I'm also not the best at describing who I am, specially because of my idea of selfishness, but I'm certain if you were to group up 20 acquaintances of mine in a room you'd get a pretty good view of who I am; although good luck trying to find that many people that know me.

Nowadays I don't have many hobbies, I typically do creative writing, or listen to music during my free time, although I've recently taken an interest on modding the game Celeste. As of October 2023, I've been working on mod that pushes the boundaries of the game's core idea by implementing features like anti-gravity and skill-based puzzles to in order to offer the player more a more intricate gameplay using mechanics similar to games like VVVVVV and Portal.

I'm a native Spanish and English speaker, and I also have fundamental awareness in Russian.

work

I'm currently working at Northern, a one-man company offering IT services for small companies in the Valley of Mexico area. I am available for hire (both independently and as part of Northern).

I also write professionally. I've worked (and still do) in multiple projects related to public transport; one of my most notable projects is Plan Estudiante (Project Garibaldi), a collaborative-writing editorial project containing a series of "booklets" surrounding Mexico City and it's various public transport systems, trying to highlight key issues with them and offering solutions.

Aside from public transport, I also write articles about personal finances, engineering, and civil aviation in/for multiple platforms and outlets.

In the past I've been involved in many projects and teams, some like Ameliorated, a project aiming to offer a more privacy-oriented Windows 10/11 installation. Others The Hitogata Search Team, a community-effort trying to find a lost Japanese PSA from the 80s. And some like Estrelar, a startup/group working on aerospace attempting to expand possible uses of weather balloons on emergency situations, and also trying to make Laika 2.0 by sending a rat to space.

I also have experience in professional video editing, working as a freelance video editor from 2020 to 2021, in which I worked with clients of many ranges, from small local coffee shops to the biggest telecommunications provider in Sweden.
